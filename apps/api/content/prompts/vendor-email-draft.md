    - What I need from them: [THE CONCRETE ASK AND A DEADLINE]

    concede fault.

## When to use this

Routine vendor correspondence where you know the facts but drafting from
scratch is slow. Not for legal disputes — those go through your contracts
office, not an AI draft.

## How to adapt it

- Fill all four context lines; the "relationship" line is what keeps the
  tone right.
- For escalations, add "this is our second follow-up" to the context.

## What to check

- PO numbers, amounts, and dates — transcribe them yourself, don't let the
  draft restate them from memory.
- Remove any commitment you didn't intend to make.
