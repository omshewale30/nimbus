## What it is

Microsoft 365 Copilot is the AI assistant embedded in the Office apps you
already use. Because it runs inside UNC's Microsoft 365 tenant, prompts and
documents are **not** used to train Microsoft's models and stay within the
university's data boundary.

## Approved uses in F&O

- Summarizing and drafting documents, emails, and meeting notes
- Analyzing spreadsheet data (see the [Excel budget variance
  playbook](/p/copilot-excel-budget-variance))
- Building first-draft presentations from existing documents

## Not approved

- Any workflow involving data classified above moderate sensitivity —
  see [What data can I put into Copilot?](/g/sensitive-data)

## Getting access

Licenses are managed by ITS. Ask your supervisor to request one through the
ITS service portal (link above).
